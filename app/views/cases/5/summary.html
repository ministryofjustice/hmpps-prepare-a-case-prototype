Offences

Fail to provide specimen for analysis - vehicle driver

On Monday 17-February-2020 at Red lane, Sheffield when suspected of having driven a vehicle and having been required to provide a specimen or specimens of breath for analysis by means of a device type approved by the secretary of state pursuant to section 3a, 4, or 5a thereof, failed, without reasonable excuse to do so.

Contrary to section 7(6) of the Road Traffic Act 1988 and Schedule 2 to the Road Traffic Offenders Act 1988.

Drive a motor vehicle otherwise than in accordance with a license - endorsable offence

On Monday 17 February 2020 at Red lane, Sheffield drove a motor vehicle, namely Kawazaki Vroom A103 ZZZ on a road, namely Red lane, Sheffield otherwise than in accordance with a licence authorising you to drive a motor vehicle of that class

Contrary to section 87(1) of the Road Traffic Act 1988 and Schedule 2 to the Road Traffic Offenders Act 1988

Use a motor vehicle on a road / public place without third party insurance

On Monday 17 February 2020 at Red lane, Sheffield used a motor vehicle namely Kawazaki Vroom A103 ZZZ on a road, or other public place, namely Red lane, Sheffield ,when there was not in force in relation to that use such a policy of insurance or such a security in respect of third party risks as complied with the requirements of part VI of the road traffic act 1988

Contrary to section 143 of the Road Traffic Act 1988 and Schedule 2 to the Road Traffic Offenders Act 1988
